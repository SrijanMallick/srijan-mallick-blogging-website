How PW Avoided BYJU'S Mistakes

A comparative strategy study of Physics Wallah's disciplined growth versus the expansion pitfalls that destabilized BYJU'S in Indian EdTech.

Context

Indian EdTech experienced explosive pandemic-era growth followed by a sharp correction. BYJU'S became the cautionary tale of aggressive acquisition, unsustainable spending, and product-market misalignment — while Physics Wallah emerged as a contrasting model of capital efficiency and focused execution.

Key Focus Areas

  • Competitive analysis between PW and BYJU'S
  • Business model design and unit economics
  • Strategic growth vs. growth-at-all-costs
  • Brand trust and exam-prep product-market fit

Strategic Takeaways

The case examines how PW's community-led, affordability-focused approach aligned with student needs in test preparation — and why BYJU'S diversification and acquisition strategy eroded focus, margins, and organizational stability.
